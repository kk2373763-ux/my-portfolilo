About
Who I Am

I am a VFX and Animation student at CMR University. I am passionate about cinematic storytelling, horror aesthetics, psychological tension, and immersive sci-fi narratives. Every frame I create is a deliberate act of atmosphere.

I founded DARKUSHION STUDIOS as an independent cinematic production identity. I build visual worlds that breathe in shadow. Darkness, in my work, is not absence. It is presence.
